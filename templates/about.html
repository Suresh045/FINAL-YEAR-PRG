National Housing Monitoring System

The National Housing Monitoring System is an initiative by the Government of India to modernize and digitize the process of housing approvals, construction tracking, and urban development monitoring across the country.

Our platform provides a user-friendly portal for citizens, developers, and administrators to manage housing-related requests efficiently. With real-time satellite image integration, AI-based construction detection, and location tracking, the system ensures transparency, accountability, and faster decision-making in housing development.

We aim to streamline the approval process, reduce manual paperwork, and provide a digital one-stop solution to improve the overall housing experience for both government officials and citizens.
